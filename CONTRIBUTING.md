# Contributing to OctoPal

Thanks for thinking about helping!

This is an early experiment, so the best ways to contribute are:

- Try chatting with OctoPal and tell me what feels confusing or could be better (open an issue!)
- Suggest safety rules or analogies that help beginners
- Point out any bugs or edge cases

Just open an issue with your idea — no pull request needed yet.

Thanks for being part of the journey! 😊
